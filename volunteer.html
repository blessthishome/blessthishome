Volunteer with Bless This Home

Thank you for giving your time and talents to the ministry of Bless This Home. We appreciate the chance to get to know you and work alongside you to serve women and families in our area who could use our help.

Wear sturdy and comfortable shoes and layered clothing. Please do not wear jewelry that you care to lose or bring personal belongings into the space. Keep them locked in your car.

We often have fun jobs to do, and sometimes there are some not so fun tasks to accomplish. Keeping a positive attitude and remembering with gratitude our own lives of relative ease will help get through the necessary tasks.

Be very mindful of social conversation while working in the space, especially if the client is there. Avoid conversations about money, spending, travel, and do not discuss the client's choices, decisions, style, lifestyle, or family members.

Keep a sense of humor and be flexible. Things can go awry. God has a beautiful way of taking our best efforts and working everything out for His good.

Maintain safety. We never work alone in a space. Please wait in your vehicle until another volunteer has arrived. If work ends when light is fading, walk together to vehicles and drive a volunteer to another car if needed.

When in doubt, give a shout. Call or text the Team Lead for the day with any questions.

“So, let’s not get tired of doing what is good. At just the right time we will reap a harvest of blessing if we don’t give up.” ~ Galatians 6:9 NLT

Volunteer Code of Conduct

Treat everyone with kindness, empathy, and respect regardless of background, beliefs, or circumstances.

Respect confidentiality. Do not disclose confidential information without proper authorization.

Maintain professionalism and the highest standards of integrity and ethical behavior.

Discrimination, harassment, bullying, gossip, and rumor spreading are strictly prohibited.

Communicate honestly and through appropriate channels.

Be punctual for your volunteer commitments and communicate in advance if you cannot fulfill them.

Use Bless This Home resources responsibly and only in ways aligned with the mission.

Refrain from alcohol or substance use during volunteer activities and do not volunteer while under the influence.

Familiarize yourself with and comply with Bless This Home policies and guidelines.

Embrace the values of this faith based organization and be mindful of your language and actions.

By signing below, I represent that I am at least 18 years of age, have read and understand the foregoing statements, and am competent to execute this agreement. I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to abide by the Bless This Home Volunteer Code of Conduct.

FCRA Notice and Background Investigation

In order to volunteer, four forms need to be filled out and submitted. One of these forms is permission from you for us to perform a background check.

In connection with your application to serve as a volunteer with Bless This Home, this notice is intended to inform you that a consumer report may be obtained on you from a consumer reporting agency for volunteer service purposes. These purposes may include placement, retention, reassignment, or continued service. The report may contain information relating to criminal information or history, driving and motor vehicle records, verification of education or employment history, social media, or other background checks.

Authorization for Background Investigation
