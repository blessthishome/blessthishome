About Us

Furnishing the home, filling the heart.

Our Mission
Our mission is to be the hands and feet of Jesus, welcoming vulnerable women and children. Through faith filled service, we witness to God's love by creating warm, dignified homes where families can heal and grow by His grace.
Our Vision
We envision a world where vulnerable women and children step into new beginnings, welcomed into warm, supportive homes rooted in the love of Jesus. In these sacred spaces, they walk in faith, are embraced by community, and are empowered to flourish through His grace.
A Message From Suzie
Years ago, I worked with underserved women and saw a need for home furnishings and decor for those moving out of restorative programs and building new lives. After some time in the design and home staging business, I decided to use my skills in a different way. I am excited to be surrounded by friends and peers who have hearts to serve God by helping their neighbors.

We have joined forces to gather local donations and transform new living spaces into functional and welcoming homes. Do you like organizing, designing, or volunteering? We are always looking for others to join us.
