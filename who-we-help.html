Who We Help

Supporting women and children stepping into independent living.

Who Do We Help?
Bless This Home supports women and children who have completed restorative programs that have lasted 6 months or longer, and are transitioning to independent living in their local communities.
Organizations We Work With
  • New Life Home for Women and Children
  • Families In Transition, Transitional Supportive Housing
  • Marguerite's Place
  • St. Gianna's Place
  • Front Door Agency
  • Light of Life
  • Family Promise
  • Step Up, Step Down
Bless This Home partners with organizations serving vulnerable women and children in transition, helping transform empty spaces into welcoming homes.
