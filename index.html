Our Mission

Bless This Home serves families by helping furnish homes with dignity, care and practical support. The goal is simple: turn empty spaces into homes that feel safe, welcoming and loved.

Furniture and Home Decor
Families need the basics that make a house feel like home, from sofas and beds to lamps, rugs, wall art and kitchen tables.
Household Necessities
Useful everyday items matter too, including kitchen supplies, shower curtains, towels, linens, vacuums and storage bins.
Financial Giving
Financial donations help Bless This Home respond quickly to needs, fill gaps and support families with the practical items they need most.
Volunteer Support
Volunteers help the ministry move, organize, receive and prepare donations so more homes can be furnished with care.

Donation Details

Donations are done by appointment only. Bless This Home receives drop offs at the warehouse located at 20 Blaine Street in Manchester, New Hampshire, on Wednesdays from 10:00am to noon and 6:00pm to 8:00pm and on the last Saturday of the month from 10:00am to noon.
