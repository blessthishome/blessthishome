Testimonials

Stories of homes transformed and lives encouraged.

Annabelle, Adonia, and Talia
“My name is Annabelle Bonner and I have a 9 year old daughter named Adonia DeWolfe. We recently moved into our new apartment with just the bare essentials like clothing, shoes, her toys, and very little items to furnish our new apartment. We were connected to Bless This Home from our previous family at Marguerite's Place. I didn't realize that decorating and furnishing the apartment was going to happen so quickly and so beautifully.

I spoke with Suzie, met with 2 beautiful ladies at our new place, they asked a few questions, took down some measurements and some notes, and the outcome was a beautiful miracle. Exploring each room was eye watering for me, specially my daughters room. The one thing she wanted more than anything this Christmas was the ice cream truck with all the little fixings that came with it. To see her run over to it and play with it with a radiance of happiness instantly brought tears to my eyes.

The one thing I prayed for and wanted more than anything was a Christmas tree where my oldest daughter, Talia and her little sister could open their presents together on Christmas Day. So, from the bottom of our hearts I sincerely would like to thank you for being part of our Circle of Angels and also being part of our Miracle Christmas.”

Thank you so much and God Bless.
